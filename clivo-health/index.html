How Clivo works

Care in three unhurried steps.

No apps to figure out, no forms to print. Tell us what's going on, meet your doctor, and get everything you need afterwards — all in one place.

Book in a minute

Pick a specialty or start with a general physician. Choose “as soon as possible” or a time that suits you, and jot down your symptoms.

Meet your doctor

Your e-prescription, notes and any lab tests land in the app. Order medicines, book a home sample pickup, and plan a follow-up in a tap.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is Clivo suitable for emergencies?

No. Clivo is for non-emergency care. If you have chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, a serious injury or any life-threatening symptom, please call your local emergency number or go to the nearest hospital right away.

Are the prescriptions valid?

Yes — every e-prescription is issued by a registered doctor after your consultation and is shared securely in the app. You can order medicines against it or take it to any pharmacy you like.

What can I be treated for online?

Common illnesses, ongoing conditions, mental health, skin concerns, general questions and reviews all work well over video. If your doctor feels you need an in-person exam or a test, they'll tell you and help you arrange it. When in doubt, book a consultation and let a doctor advise what's right for you.

How private is my information?

Your consultations and records are encrypted and only accessible to you and your care team. We never sell your data. You can request a copy of your records or ask us to delete them at any time.

Which cities does Clivo serve?

Our doctors consult with people across India by video. Home lab collection and medicine delivery are currently focused around Bengaluru, with more neighbourhoods being added.
