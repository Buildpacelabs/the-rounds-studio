Good to know

Questions before you book

Do I need a doctor's referral to book?

No — you can book an assessment directly with us. If you're coming after surgery or a scan, bring any reports along and we'll factor them into your plan.

What happens in the first assessment?

A conversation about your history and goals, a movement and strength screen, a hands-on assessment, and a plan you both agree on. Many people begin treatment in the same visit.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing you can move in. For lower-limb issues, shorts are helpful; for shoulders or neck, a vest or loose top works well.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on the injury and your goals. We'll give you an honest estimate after the first assessment and keep reviewing it as you progress.

Do you help with recovery after surgery?

Yes. We work alongside your surgeon's guidance to rebuild range, strength and function at a safe, sensible pace.
