What we treat

Three things we do
exceptionally well.

We keep a deliberately focused practice — joints, spine and sports injuries — so every case is handled by a consultant who does this work day in, day out.

Joint replacement

Hips & knees, built to move

When a joint is worn out, the right replacement can give back years of comfortable movement. We plan each one to your anatomy and your goals — and use image-guided, tissue-sparing techniques where they help.

  • Knee replacement
  • Hip replacement
  • Partial (uni) knee
  • Revision surgery
  • Cartilage repair
Discuss a joint
Spine

Necks & backs, calmed

Most back and neck pain settles without an operation. We start conservative — with a clear diagnosis, targeted therapy and injections where useful — and reserve minimally invasive surgery for when it is genuinely the right call.

  • Back & neck pain
  • Sciatica
  • Disc problems
  • Spinal injections
  • Keyhole spine surgery
Talk about your back
Sports injury

Back on the field, safely

Ligament, tendon and joint injuries need a plan that respects your sport and your timeline. We combine keyhole surgery with structured return-to-play rehab, so you come back ready — not just cleared.

  • ACL reconstruction
  • Shoulder instability
  • Arthroscopy (keyhole)
  • Tendon injuries
  • Return-to-play

Why Pivot

Careful with the knife.
Serious about recovery.

Good orthopaedics is as much about the operations we talk you out of as the ones we do. Here's what shapes how we work.

Image-guided precision

Modern imaging and, where it helps, computer-assisted planning — for accurate, tissue-sparing surgery.

Day-care & keyhole

Many procedures done through small incisions, often as day-care — so recovery starts sooner and gentler.

Rehab under one roof

In-house physiotherapy means your recovery plan is joined up with your surgery from day one.

Honest second opinions

Clear, unhurried advice. If surgery isn't the right answer for you, we'll tell you plainly.
