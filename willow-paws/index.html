Everything under one leafy roof

Care for every life stage, from first jab to golden years

Whether it's a wriggly new puppy, a cat who hates the carrier, or an old friend who needs a little extra comfort — we meet each pet where they are.

Wellness & vaccinations

Annual health checks, core and lifestyle vaccines, deworming and nutrition advice — all on a schedule that keeps your pet happily protected.

Puppy & kitten starter plans

Preventive check-ups

Weight, teeth, ears, skin and heart — a gentle nose-to-tail once-over that catches the small things before they grow.

Dental care

Scaling, polishing and gentle extractions under monitored anaesthesia, plus at-home advice to keep breath fresh and gums healthy.

Bad breath is worth a look

Surgery & spay/neuter

Routine and soft-tissue procedures in a calm theatre, with careful pain relief, close monitoring and a call from us the same evening.

Full pre-anaesthetic checks

Diagnostics & imaging

In-house bloods, digital X-ray and ultrasound mean faster answers and less waiting when something isn't quite right.

Senior & chronic care

Arthritis, kidney and thyroid support, weight plans and comfort-first check-ins for the pets who've been with you the longest.
