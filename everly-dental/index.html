Book a consultation

Dental emergency? Call +91 93157 76817 — we keep same-day slots free for urgent pain most days.

I'm genuinely scared of the dentist. Can you help?
Absolutely — it's a big part of why Everly exists. Tell us at booking that you're nervous and we'll build in extra time, use tell-show-do, agree a stop signal, and offer calming options. You set the pace, always.
What happens at a first visit?
A friendly chat about your history and worries, a gentle exam with a digital scan, and a clear picture of anything worth watching. You'll leave with written options — no treatment gets done without your yes.
Aligners or braces — which is right for me?
It depends on your teeth and goals. Clear aligners suit many cases and are near-invisible; braces can be better for complex movements. We'll assess and recommend what genuinely fits you — book a consultation for advice specific to your smile.
Do you see children?
Yes — we love first visits. We keep them short, playful and pressure-free so kids build a happy relationship with the chair early on.
How much will treatment cost?
Every mouth is different, so we give you a written, itemised estimate after your exam — before anything starts. No hidden add-ons, and consultation fees are confirmed when you book.
Where are you, and is parking easy?
We're on 100 Feet Road, Indiranagar, Bengaluru — a short walk from the metro, with two-wheeler and car parking nearby. Full directions come with your appointment confirmation.
