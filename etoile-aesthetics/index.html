Our belief

Aesthetic surgery, done well, should be quiet. People notice that you look rested — not what you had done.

Étoile was founded on a simple idea: the best result is one that looks like you, on a good day. We are conservative by temperament. We would rather talk you out of a procedure than perform one you do not need.

Every plan is shaped around your anatomy, your reasons and your recovery — never a trend or a target. You will always leave a consultation knowing exactly what is realistic, and what is not.

“First, do no harm to what already works.” The Étoile approach

The considered approach

Four unhurried steps, start to finish

A calm, transparent path with no obligation to proceed. You set the pace; we make sure you are informed at every stage.

  1. 01

    Consultation in confidence

    An unhurried conversation about what you would like to change and why. We assess, we listen, and we are honest about what surgery or treatment can — and cannot — do.

  2. 02

    A considered plan

    A written plan tailored to your anatomy and goals, with realistic expectations, recovery times and any alternatives set out plainly. Take it home. There is never pressure to decide.

  3. 03

    Procedure & attentive care

    Carried out in accredited facilities with experienced anaesthetic and nursing teams. Comfort, safety and dignity are looked after from admission to discharge.

  4. 04

    Recovery & follow-up

    Structured aftercare, clear guidance and review appointments for as long as you need them. Healing well matters as much to us as the result itself.

Safety & ethics

The part we never compromise on

Aesthetic medicine is still medicine. Our standards for safety, consent and honesty do not flex for anyone.

Accredited facilities

Surgery only in licensed, fully-equipped theatres with qualified anaesthetic and nursing support on hand.

Senior surgical team

Board-certified consultants with two decades of combined experience — the surgeon you meet is the surgeon who operates.

Honest counsel

No sales targets, no package upsells. If a procedure is not right for you, we will say so.

Confidentiality by default

Discreet scheduling, private consultations and your information kept strictly between you and your care team.

Informed consent is a conversation, not a form. We use a cooling-off period for surgery so that every decision is made calmly and with full understanding of the risks and recovery involved.
