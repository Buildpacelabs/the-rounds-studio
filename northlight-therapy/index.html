What we help with

Whatever brought you here, you don't have to carry it alone.

People come to Northlight for all kinds of reasons — some clear, some harder to put into words. Here are a few of the things we work with most.

Anxiety & panic

Racing thoughts, restlessness, or that constant hum of worry that's hard to switch off. We'll help you understand it and steady it.

Low mood & depression

When days feel flat, heavy or pointless, and the things you used to enjoy don't land the way they did. There's a way back, at your pace.

Relationships & couples

Communication that's stopped working, growing apart, or the same argument on repeat. For individuals and couples, together or on your own.

Stress & burnout

Running on empty, snapping at people you love, or dreading Monday by Friday afternoon. Learn to rest — without the guilt.

Grief & loss

Losing a person, a relationship, a chapter of life. Grief has no timeline — we'll make room for yours, however it arrives.

Self-esteem & identity

The inner critic that never lets up, big life transitions, or simply feeling unsure of who you are right now. Let's find some footing.

Not sure if what you're feeling “counts”? It does. A first conversation is the easiest way to find out how we can help — book one here.

How therapy works

Four small steps, none of them a leap.

  1. 1

    Reach out

    Tell us a little about what's going on — or simply that you'd like to talk. You don't need the right words, or any words at all yet.

  2. 2

    Your first session

    A relaxed conversation, around fifty minutes, to understand you and what you're hoping for. There's no pressure to share more than you're ready to.

  3. 3

    A plan that fits you

    Together we agree an approach and a pace. Therapy isn't one-size-fits-all, and we'll shape it around your life, not the other way around.

  4. 4

    Ongoing support

    Regular sessions — in person or online — that we adjust as you go. You can pause, slow down or space things out whenever you need to.

Why Northlight

Care that starts with listening.

Northlight began with a simple idea — that good therapy shouldn't feel clinical, rushed or out of reach. Our practitioners are experienced clinical and counselling psychologists who work in warm, plain language, not jargon.

Whether this is your first time in therapy or you're returning after a break, we'll meet you exactly where you are — no assumptions, and no pressure to share more than you're ready to.
