On-site diagnostics

Tests, results and answers in one visit

You shouldn't have to run between labs to get a diagnosis. The clinic has its own sound-treated booth and scopes, so most assessments happen while you're here — and we walk you through what they mean before you leave.

  • Pure-tone audiometry
  • Tympanometry & middle-ear pressure
  • Flexible nasal & throat endoscopy
  • Video ear microscopy
  • Positional & balance testing
  • Allergy screening on referral

Why the name

To attune is to listen closely, then adjust

That's exactly how good ENT care should work. We give you unhurried time, reach for the least-invasive option that will actually do the job, and only suggest surgery when it's genuinely the right call.

  • Unhurried consultations
  • Least-invasive first
  • Plain-language plans
  • One team, start to finish

Your visit

Four unhurried steps, start to finish

  1. 01

    Book

    Pick a time online or call and speak to a person. Tell us briefly what's bothering you so we set aside the right slot.

  2. 02

    Assess

    A proper history, a careful examination, and any tests you need — hearing, endoscopy or balance — usually the same visit.

  3. 03

    Explain & plan

    We show you what we found in plain language and agree the next step together, so you leave knowing what happens and why.

  4. 04

    Follow through

    Medication, a small procedure or a review — with a clear point of contact if anything changes or you're worried.

For the whole family

From grommets to hearing aids

Children's ears and grown-up voices need different hands and a different pace. Our consultants are used to both — the wriggly five-year-old with glue ear and the grandparent who's quietly stopped joining conversations.
