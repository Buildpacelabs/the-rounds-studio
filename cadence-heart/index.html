Preventive cardiology

Most heart trouble is quieter — and more preventable — than people expect.

The best cardiology happens years before an emergency. We help you understand your own risk and act on it early, without alarm.

A first visit is a proper conversation: your symptoms, your family history, your numbers. From there we build a plan you can actually follow — and we revisit it as life changes.

  • Heart-risk assessment and family-history review
  • Blood pressure, cholesterol and rhythm screening
  • Personalised lifestyle and, where needed, medication plans
  • Clear next steps written in plain language
